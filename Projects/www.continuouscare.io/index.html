Scalable, Secure and Supported

The Virtual Practice grows with you—whether you're a solo clinic or a healthcare service startup.

Complete Support

Our support is closely connected with the product, technical, and operations teams, ensuring that every query is resolved with speed and expertise. Customers needing setup and operational support are supported by our efficient operations teams.

Multi-Language Support

Patient interfaces support multiple languages, ensuring the right experience for your users. We work with you to deliver in the languages your patients need.

Security & Compliance

All data is encrypted with regular backups and continuous security monitoring. HIPAA and GDPR compliance, along with built-in patient consent, ensure your practice stays protected by default.

Regional Clouds

The ContinuousCare platform is hosted on regional clouds in the US, EU, India, and Singapore. Your Virtual Practice and patient data are securely hosted in the region that matches your country of registration. Enterprise customers can also opt for dedicated cloud installations

Real White-labelling

While custom branding and domain mapping are supported for all customers, full white-labelling is also supported including publishing your mobile apps in your own Google and Apple app store accounts.

APIs

Use the platform APIs to securely access data in your Virtual Practice and support core actions like creating patients and booking appointments. Designed for safe integrations, with more capabilities planned over time.
